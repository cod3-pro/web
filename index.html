How does it work?

  • Choose a template

    First of all, you are required to choose the template, either from the templates given on this site, other linked sites or by providing your sketch, figma or psd.

  • Forward your choice to us

    Once you have chosen the template, forward its link or template number to us along with content (.zip file) that needs to be replaced within the website.

  • Finalization & Uploading

    During web design and development, we will be in close coordination and be updating you about progress. Once revisions and content insertion is done you will be shown the website on local server. After its final approval, you will be requested to pay the due amount and website will be uploaded to your domain and hosting.
